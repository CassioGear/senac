ABOUT US

Find your fandom at Funko, a hub for pop culture. Bring more fun into your everyday with your favorite fandoms and characters as a variety of collectible figures, apparel, accessories, plush, action figures, digital collectibles, and more. Funko has the world’s largest selection of pop culture licenses and is the perfect place to find the next piece for your collection or gifts.

Funko also fuels the Loungefly, Mondo, and Digital Pop! brands. Loungefly specializes in bringing your fandom to life through your wardrobe and accessories. Those who love music, poster art, and scale action figures will love Mondo’s selection. Fans of digital collectibles will find many of the same pop culture fandoms as Digital Pop! via the Droppp platform. Make each day happier with the things you love the most!
